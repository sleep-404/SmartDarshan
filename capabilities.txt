## Accuracy Risk Matrix

| Analytics | Target | Risk Level | Mitigation |
|-----------|--------|------------|------------|
| Crowd density | ≥80% | Low | Mature tech, multiple model options |
| Peak prediction | ≥80% | Low | 5 years data, clear patterns |
| Chatbot accuracy | ≥80% | Medium | Telugu STT needs validation |
| Fall detection | ≥75% | Medium | Pose estimation at 4MP may struggle |
| Stampede detection | ≥70% | High | Limited training data for temple context |
| Elderly gait | ≥70% | High | Requires custom model training |
| Emotion (facial) | ≥75% | Medium | Only for frontal feedback videos |
